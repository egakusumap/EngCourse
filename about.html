why choose our course?

Ndol course is an English language course and training institution which was founded in 2013 with the spirit of quality education for all groups. The Ndol course applies the Exclusive Method learning system, which is a combination of superior methods applied at many well-known institutions. We call it the Exclusive method because the combination of superior methods is only applied in the Ndol course. Where the combination of fun learning and quality learning is applied in a unified and systematic manner. Apart from that, we also limit the number of participants per class, namely an average of 10 people. This has a positive impact on the quality of teaching and the classroom atmosphere. Supported by professional teaching staff, superior learning facilities and a complete curriculum, participants will be able to digest each instructor's presentation well. Apart from that, applicable and modern learning media also makes it easier for participants to master the material. Currently, the Ndol course program has been attended by more than 15,000 course participants.

Highly qualified teachers

Studies show that teacher quality is the most important learning-related factor that influences progress. That is why not just anyone can become an Teacher Ndol course . We choose them with care and train them to be the highest quality online English teachers available
